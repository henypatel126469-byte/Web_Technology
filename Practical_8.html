Vision

To be a leading institution of higher education recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and commitment to the holistic development of students who contribute meaningfully to society.

Mission

  • To inspire students to innovate, excel, and contribute to society through a dynamic learning environment that nurtures creativity collaboration, and entrepreneurship.
  • To foster intellectual curiosity, ethical values, and social responsibility among students, helping them evolve into confident professionals and compassionate human beings.
  • Provide skills, knowledge, and attitudes for success as engineers, leaders, and entrepreneurs.
  • To educate, empower, and elevate every student for success in life and service to society.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  1. Apply analytical and problem-solving skills to innovate and contribute to research, entrepreneurship, and sustainable technological development.
  2. Apply fundamental principles of computer science to design and develop software and hardware solutions for real-world problems. Adapt to emerging technological advancements including cybersecurity, AR/VR, and IoT.
