Vision

Build a strong research and teaching environment that responds swiftly to the challenges of the 21st century.

Mission

  • Commits to excellence in teaching, research, and entrepreneurial training.
  • Builds engineers who are socially sensitive, uphold professional ethics, can engage in R&D with industries and global partners, and are lifelong learners.
  • Delivers quality technical education using contemporary curricula, emphasizing leadership, ethics, and integrity.
  • Modern teaching methodologies and infrastructure

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  1. “Learn and become skilled in new fields such as Big Data, Cloud Computing, AI, Robotics, Data Analytics, IoT, and Machine Learning to improve your ability to solve problems.”
  2. Faster continuous learning by integrating research and industry trends, ensuring relevance and agility in evolving tech landscapes.
